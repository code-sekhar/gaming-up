GAME OVER VIEW

In Battle of Wrath, players are transported to a fantasy world where they must engage in sword battles to achieve glory and earn rewards. With a focus on player-driven economics and decentralized asset ownership, Battle of Wrath provides a unique gaming experience that is both engaging and rewarding. In Battle of Wrath, players start by creating their own unique characters, each with unique abilities and attributes. Players can then engage in sword battles against one another in realtime, with the winner taking the spoils of war. The goal of the game is to rise to the top of the leaderboard and become the ultimate sword master.
